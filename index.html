Ramanujan Summation :


Sum of series is always a interesting fact in mathematics.When this is about a "Divergent Seies",it seems to be ridiculous. But, Srinivash Ramanujan, the great Indian Mathematician, tried to figure out this fact. He invented a technique for assigning a value to a divergent series. And, this method is referred to as Ramanujan Summation. His findings astonied the world mathematicians.He fixed a value "-1/12" to the series of natural numbers.

1 + 2 + 3 + 4 +........+ N +......... = -1/12
